Myles Borins is a Toronto-based product leader working across developer platforms, data, infrastructure, and AI. He currently works on Developer Platform at Snowflake, helping shape the tools and systems people use to build pipelines, manage data, and work with agents and AI.

Before Snowflake, he worked at GitHub, Google, and IBM. He has spent much of his career around developer tools, open source, and the JavaScript and Node.js ecosystem.

Outside of work, he has spent years making things on the internet through software, music, and community.
